# Product Definition: Target Customer Segments

This document defines the primary customer segments for the product, including their characteristics, calendar requirements, and strategic positioning.

---

## 1. Beauty & Aesthetics

*Primary competitor territory: Booksy*

Highest no-show impact in this vertical. Customers strongly value appointment reminders and confirmation flows.


| Business Type                | Calendars | Notes                           |
| ---------------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------- |
| Barber shops / Hair salons   | 1–3       | One per chair or professional   |
| Beauty centers / Nail salons | 2–5       | Multiple cabins or workstations |
| Tattoo studios               | 1–4       | One per artist                  |


**Average calendars per client:** 2.5

---

## 2. Health & Wellness

*High-trust vertical*

Customers prioritize reliability and professionalism. WhatsApp reminders and confirmations are highly valued.


| Business Type                 | Calendars | Notes                               |
| ----------------------------- | --------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Physiotherapists / Osteopaths | 1–3       | Often 1–2 freelancers sharing space |
| Psychologists / Nutritionists | 1         | Usually individual practitioners    |
| Small dental clinics          | 2–4       | Dentist, hygienist, surgeon         |
| Veterinarians                 | 2–3       | Simultaneous consultation rooms     |


**Average calendars per client:** 1.8

---

## 3. Professional Services & Others

*Underserved segment — many businesses discover the need when they see WhatsApp saves 2+ hours of phone time per day.*


| Business Type                   | Calendars | Notes                        |
| ------------------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------- |
| Mechanics / Car washes          | 1–2       | One per bay or wash line     |
| Private tutors / Academies      | 3–8       | One per teacher or classroom |
| Padel courts / Sports centers   | 2–6       | One per court                |
| Real estate (property viewings) | 2–5       | One per sales agent          |


**Average calendars per client:** 3.2
